Diploma Level

Diploma courses are MQF Level 4 awards, offered by universities, polytechnics and accredited private providers.

These awards are available in both the higher education and vocational sectors. The Diploma programmes require the completion of at least 90 credits in the credit system.

Bachelor's Degree

A bachelor's degree typically takes three to four years to complete, though some fields, like medicine and architecture, may require five or more years.

This program offers a comprehensive education, providing students with specialized knowledge in their major while also requiring courses in social sciences and humanities. This well-rounded approach aims to develop critical thinking, analysis, and maturity in students.

Post-Graduate

Post-graduate studies follow the completion of a bachelor's degree and include master's and doctoral programs.

These courses allow students to engage in more in-depth research within their chosen fields. Generally, a master's degree requires a recognized bachelor's degree, while a doctorate typically necessitates a master's degree, although some exceptional bachelor's degree holders may apply directly for doctoral studies.

FAQ

How do I register for classes?

You can register for classes by logging into your student portal and navigating to the registration section. Follow the prompts to select your desired courses.

What are the requirements for enrollment?

The enrollment requirements vary by program, but generally include proof of previous education, identification documents, and any specific prerequisites outlined for your chosen course.
