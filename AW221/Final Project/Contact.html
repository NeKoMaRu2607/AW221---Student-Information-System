About Us

Founded in 2019, Pinnacle Academy aims to provide exceptional education and nurture future leaders. Our dedicated team of educators and industry professionals emphasizes academic excellence and holistic development. In 2024, we received accreditation from the Ministry of Higher Education (MoHE) Malaysia, reflecting our commitment to high educational standards. With a diverse student body and experienced faculty, we are dedicated to shaping the future of our students and contributing to national growth.
